Lesson 1

Vocabulary
  1. wo: I, me      
  2. ni: you      
  3. ni hao: hello      
  4. ta: he, she      
  5. shi: is, are      
  6. shei: who      
  7. hao: good      
  8. tang lao shi: Teacher Tom      
Dialogue
  1. English: Who are you?
    Pin Yin: Ni shi shei?
    Characters: ?
     
  2. English: Hello! I am Teacher Tom. Who are you?
    Pin Yin: Ni hao! Wo shi tang lao shi. Ni shi shei?
    Characters: !. ?
     
  3. English: Hello Teacher Tom, I am Felix
    Pin Yin: Tang lao shi ni haowo shi Felix.
    Characters: , Felix.
     
  4. English: Hello Felix. Who is that?
    Pin Yin: Felix, ni hao. Ta shi shei?
    Characters: Felix, . ?
     
  5. English: She is Carolina
    Pin Yin: Ta sh Carolina.
    Characters: Carolina.
     
  6. English: Hello Carolina
    Pin Yin: Carolina ni hao.
    Characters: Carolina.
     
Lesson 1 Review
  1. Who is Tang lao shi?

     
  2. Translate to Chinese: "Hello, I am Alex. Who are you?"

     
  3. What does lao shi mean?

     
  4. Which means "you"?
    A) wo
    B) ni
    C) ta

     
  5. Translate to English: "Ni shi Bailey. Ta shi Morgan."

Lesson 2

Vocabulary
  1. dong xi: stuff, thing      
  2. xin: new      
  3. tong xue: classmate      
  4. lai: to come      
  5. he: and      
  6. bu zhi dao: do not know      
  7. ma: put it at the end of a sentence to make the sentence a question      
  8. ye: also, too      
Dialogue
  1. English: Ming Mings new classmate is Ben Ben.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming de xin tong xue shi Ben Ben
    Characters:.
     
  2. English: Ming Mings new classmate has arrived for class.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming de xin tong xue lai shang ke.
    Characters:.
     
  3. English: Ming Ming and Ben Ben take Ms. Mings Chinese class.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming he Ben Ben shang Ming lao shi de zhong wen ke.
    Characters: .
     
  4. English: Ms. Ming: Whose stuff is this?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Zhe shi shei de dong xi?
    Characters: : ?
     
  5. English: Ming Ming: Its not mine. I dont know whose stuff it is.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Bu shi wo de. Wo bu zhi dao shi shei de dong xi.
    Characters: : .
     
  6. English: Ms. Ming: Ben Ben, is this your stuff?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Ben Benzhe shi ni de dong xi ma?
    Characters: : ?
     
  7. English: Ben Ben: Its not mine. I also dont know whose stuff it is.
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Bu shi wo dewo ye bu zhi dao shi shei de dong xi.
    Characters: : .
     
  8. English: Ms. Ming: Is this Mr. Toms stuff?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Zhe shi Tang lao shi de dong xi ma?
    Characters: : ?
     
  9. English: Ben Ben: Its not Mr. Toms either.
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ye bu shi Tang lao shi de.
    Characters: : .
     
  10. English: Ms. Ming: Whose stuff is this?
    Pin Yin: Ming lao shi: Zhe shi shei de?!
    Characters: : ?!
     
Lesson 2 Review
  1. What does the opposite of "zhi dao" mean?

     
  2. Translate to Chinese: "This is not John's stuff."

     
  3. Define tong xue.

     
  4. What is the function of the word ma?

     
  5. Translate to English: "Wo ye bu zhi dao shi shei de dong xi."

Vocabulary

Lesson 4

Vocabulary
  1. yi ge: a, one, an      
  2. ha: ha(sound of laughter)      
  3. ren: person      
  4. yi si: meaning or definition      
  5. hai: still      
  6. xiao: laughing, smiling      
  7. ming ming: clearly, obviously      
  8. yu zhou: the universe      
  9. lai: from, to come from      
  10. hao: good, very      
  11. gao xiao: funny      
  12. yi ge tong xue: a/one classmate      
  13. yi ge lao shi: a/one teacher      
  14. yi ge han zi: a/one Chinese character      
  15. yi ge peng you: a/one friend      
  16. yi ge dong xi: a/one thing      
Dialogue
  1. English: Ben Ben: Are you a thing?
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ni shi yi ge dong xi ma?
    Characters: : ?
     
  2. English: Ming Ming: No! Im not a thing, Im a person!
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Bu shi! Wo bu shi yi ge dong xi wo shi yi ge ren!
    Characters: : ! , !
     
  3. English: Ben Ben: Hahahaha, youre not a thing! Thats hilarious.
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ha ha ha ha, ni bu shi yi ge dong xi Hao gao xiao.
    Characters: : , ! !
     
  4. English: Ming Ming: Whats funny? Youre also not a thing. Youre also a person.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Shen me gao xiao? Ni ye bu shi yi ge dong xi. Ni ye shi yi ge ren.
    Characters: : ? . .
     
  5. Enlish: Ben Ben: Do you know what: I am not a thing means?
    Pin Yin: Ben Ben: Ni zhi dao "w b shi yi g dong xi" de yi si ma?
    Characters: : : "" ?
     
  6. English: Ming Ming: Youre still laughing. I clearly dont know.
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Ni hai xiao. Wo ming ming bu zhi dao.
    Characters: : , .
     
  7. English: I am not a thing means: I am not from this Universe. I clearly am not a person.
    Pin Yin: "Wo bu shi yi ge dong xi" De yi si shi: "Wo bu shi zhe ge yu zhou lai de. Wo ming ming bu shi yi ge ren."
    Characters: : "" : ". ."
     
  8. English: Hahahaha, thats funny!
    Pin Yin: Ming Ming: Ha ha ha ha, hao gao xiao!
    Characters: : , !
     
Lesson 4 Review
  1. How do you say very funny in Chinese?

     
  2. Zen me yi si tong xue ma?

     
  3. Define hao.

     
  4. How do you say one, a, or an?

     
  5. We live someplace very large with galaxies, planets, moons, and more. What do you call it in Chinese?
